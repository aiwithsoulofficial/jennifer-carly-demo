Got questions?

Everything you need to know

Does it hurt?

Not really. We apply numbing cream beforehand so you're comfortable throughout. Most people describe the sensation as warm and tingly - not painful.

How long until I see results?

Most clients notice some tightening in the first week. The main results build progressively from around 8-12 weeks as your skin produces new collagen.

Will I need time off work?

Most people are back to normal the next day. There may be some mild redness or swelling in the area, but nothing that requires downtime for most clients.

What if I want to treat other areas too?

Totally up to you. This offer covers the under-chin area. If you'd like to talk about other areas after your appointment, we're happy to chat - but there's no pressure at all.

How is this different from surgery?

No cutting, no general anaesthetic, no significant recovery time. You walk in, we treat the area, you walk out. The technology works by stimulating your skin's own collagen - not by removing tissue.

Is the deposit refundable?

Yes. If you cancel 48 hours or more before your appointment, your $100 deposit is fully refunded. If you go ahead, it's applied directly to your $349 treatment cost.
